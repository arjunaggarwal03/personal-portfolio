*Forthcoming.* The thesis: a chat window is the wrong primitive. Useful agents
need a harness: tools, durable workflow state, permissions, human-in-the-loop
review, notifications, and feedback loops. This essay will walk through each
layer and why the harness, not the model, is where the product lives.
